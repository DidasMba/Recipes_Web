Congolese Beef Recipe

A classic African recipe that’s become a firm favourite. This recipe serves 8-10 people and is great for an informal dinner party or family get -together.

Recipe Ingredients

  • 250 g SPAR Lasagne Sheets
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 125 g SPAR Streaky Bacon, finely chopped

Recipe Method

  1. In a large heavy based saucepan, sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until translucent. Add bacon and mince and fry until lightly browned. Stir in all remaining meat sauce ingredients

  2. Bring to the boil then simmer, covered for 1 hour. Check seasoning. For the cheese sauce, melt butter in a saucepan.
  3. Stir in flour and cook for 1 minute.
